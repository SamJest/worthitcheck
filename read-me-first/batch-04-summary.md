# WorthItCheck Batch 04

Paste these files into the site root on top of Batches 01 to 03.

## What this batch changes
- Improves the homepage as a routing page, not just a single-tool landing page.
- Adds a new **Choose the right path** section on the homepage so visitors can reach the most relevant tool or hub faster.
- Improves the tools index with clearer category routing for repair, upgrade, timing, and ownership questions.
- Adds stronger cross-cluster sections on the three public guide hubs so visitors do not dead-end after reading one cluster page.
- Tightens several collection-page titles and meta descriptions to better describe what each hub actually covers.
- Adds new reusable routing-card styles for future batches.
