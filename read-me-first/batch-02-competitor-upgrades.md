# WorthItCheck batch 02 competitor-upgrade pack

This pack is arranged so you can paste it directly into the project root.

## What this batch does

This batch applies as many of the next-step competitor-beating improvements as fit cleanly into the current static structure:

1. Adds search-first guide hubs around the strongest commercial/tool intents:
   - rent vs buy
   - lease or buy car
   - new or used car
   - buy or wait

2. Adds new long-tail guide pages aimed at the narrower searches competitors tend to capture:
   - rent vs buy: first-time buyer, short stay, low deposit, high mortgage rates, service-charge flat
   - lease or buy car: PCP vs lease, buy outright vs finance, high-mileage driver, EV lease vs buy
   - new or used car: reliability, short ownership, depreciation hit, replace current car
   - buy or wait: Black Friday, iPhone timing, Samsung Galaxy timing, laptop timing
   - trade-in phone: private sale vs trade-in, broken phone trade-in, best time to trade in

3. Strengthens internal linking:
   - homepage now promotes the new guide clusters
   - tools index now cleanly promotes both tools and guide clusters
   - major tool pages now link into their surrounding guide clusters

4. Fixes/cleans structure:
   - rewrites the tools index page into a cleaner hub
   - rewrites the trade-in guide hub to include the expanded cluster
   - updates the sitemap to include the expanded page set
