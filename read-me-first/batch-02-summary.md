# Batch 02

## What this batch adds

1. Adds two branded support pages:
   - `about/index.html`
   - `how-it-works/index.html`
2. Improves social/share metadata by switching pages to a real large-image OG asset instead of relying on the SVG logo alone.
3. Adds footer crawl links to `About` and `How it works` across the site so those pages are reachable from every HTML page.
4. Adds homepage and tools-index trust sections that explain the methodology and the purpose of the site.
5. Updates `sitemap.xml` to include the two new pages.

## Why this batch matters

- Strengthens brand trust for users landing cold from search.
- Gives Google clearer branded/support pages to understand the site.
- Improves internal crawl depth and distributes link equity into support pages.
- Makes social cards more reliable with a PNG OG image instead of SVG-only metadata.
