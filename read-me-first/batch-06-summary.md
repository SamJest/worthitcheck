# Batch 06 summary

This batch adds an **action layer** to all 6 live tools so users do not just get a verdict — they also get a clearer sense of what to do next.

## Included
- new **Best next move** result section on all 6 tool pages
- shared action-plan renderer in `tools/tooling.js`
- new action-plan card styling in `styles.css`
- tool-specific next-step guidance for:
  - should-i-upgrade
  - buy-or-wait
  - lease-or-buy-car
  - new-or-used-car
  - rent-vs-buy
  - trade-in-or-keep-your-phone

## Why this batch matters
The tools already explained **why** they reached a verdict. This batch makes the result more practical by answering the next question users usually have:

**"Okay, what should I actually do now?"**

That should improve perceived usefulness, trust, and time on page without adding clutter to the forms themselves.
